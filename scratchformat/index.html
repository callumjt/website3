Scratchformat

The Transition to ScratchTools

ScratchFormat is an amazing extension that let's you better customize your comments on Scratch. You can add bold, italic, and more. This doesn't work for users who don't have the extension installed, but it still looks amazing for everyone who does.

However, ScratchFormat is no longer being maintained as of late 2021. Halfway through 2022, ScratchTools decided to start maintaining ScratchFormat again, this time inside of ScratchTools itself. Now, ScratchFormat is well-maintained and a feature of ScratchTools.

All ScratchFormat users are receiving a notification. It lets them know that ScratchFormat is no longer maintained, and that they should download ScratchTools for the best experience, as it is still being maintained there. At ScratchTools, we are looking for a way to make ScratchFormat even better.

Install

Download ScratchTools today so you can use it in your very own browser!

ScratchTools is available on Chrome, Firefox, and Microsoft Edge!
